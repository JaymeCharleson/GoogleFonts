"Homenaje is inspired by the bronze cemetery art found in the Recoleta and Chacaritas districts of Buenos Aires, Argentina. A grotesque and narrow type, it provides economy in text setting without losing its STRONG, straight and steady features. The name “Homenaje” means tribute in Spanish, a modest way to honor the patient and accurate work of the lettering artists whose bronze letters honor a lifetime. Designed by *Constanza Artigas Preller and *Agustina Mingote. "
